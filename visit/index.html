Common Questions

A few things people always ask.

Do I need any experience driving a pontoon? +

Yes — we ask that you've driven a pontoon before. We'll still go over this boat's throttle, anchor, and radio when you pick up, but it isn't the place to learn from scratch. First time on a pontoon? Give us a call before you book and we'll talk it through.

Can I bring my dog? +

Yes, well-mannered dogs are welcome on the pontoon. You're responsible for any pet-related cleaning or damage, aggressive animals aren't allowed, and additional cleaning fees may apply. Give us a heads-up so we can put down an extra towel.

What happens if it rains? +

Light rain is fine, the bimini keeps you covered. For strong winds, lightning, or fog, we'll reschedule or refund, your call. We'd rather lose the rental than the boat, or you.

Is there a place to dock and grab lunch? +

A few. On Long Lake, Harbor View (east end) or Sandtrap Bar and Grill at Westwood Golf Course (west end). On Wilson, the Flyway resort (southwest end). On Solberg, Sunset Bay or the Polish Palace (both on the northeast side). Elk and Duroy don't have spots like that — they're stumpy, weedy fishing lakes, not cruising water.

Can we tow a tube or water-ski? +

Not at this time. We don't currently offer tubes, and nothing — and no one — may be towed behind the boats.

Do you do weddings, birthdays, or events? +

Yes — the pontoon seats up to 15 and makes a great spot for a celebration. Parties are welcome; our alcohol rules still apply (never the person driving). Reach out by phone, text, or email and tell us about it.
