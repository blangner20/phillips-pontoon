Book Your Day

Online booking is coming soon. Until it's live, you book with us directly. Call, text, or email us the details and we'll confirm your dates and get you on the water.

What to send us

  1. 01

    The dates you want

    A single day, or a whole stretch of them. Tell us roughly when you'd like to be on the water.

  2. 02

    Which boat

    The 25-foot pontoon or the 18-foot Ranger. Not sure? Say what you're hoping to do and we'll point you at the right one.

  3. 03

    How many people

    The pontoon carries 15. The Ranger seats five to seven.

Boats and rates are on the fleet page. Deposits, cancellations, and everything else live in rules of the lake.
