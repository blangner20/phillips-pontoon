Behind the booking

Live calendar

Booked days lock out automatically. No double-booking, no checking with you first.

Secure payment + card hold

Paid on an encrypted checkout the moment they book, with a card hold for any damage.

Tiered cancellation

97% back 14+ days out, a rental voucher inside 14 days, nothing inside 72 hours. The system runs it, not you.

Waivers auto-sent

The signing email goes out the second they book. Signed and on file before they launch.
